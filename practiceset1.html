Malerie sat on the couch and carefully looked over the pictures from the shoot. She zoomed in and out and tried different filters. It wasn’t easy to pick the best, all of them were pretty great, but finally she made a decision.

Then she opened Instagram, selected the picture she wanted and carefully typed her caption.

“Wow, guys! Thank you so much for all the birthday wishes! It feels so good to be 21!” She proceeded to add twenty-one hearts to her caption, then hit post.

“Perfect!” She smiled, and rewarded herself with another piece of birthday cake.

The Perfect Picture

A Modern Satire
